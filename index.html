Favorite GE Subject
STAT 101
Statistics was one of the subjects that I did not expect to enjoy. I was so used to statistics being a lot of computational logic to yield significant figures, but STAT 101 caught me off guard. It was more on picking the right tools to maximize your methodology's effectiveness and truthfulness, which was just incredible. Everything they taught was useful for my future machine learning endeavors since those would need to have accompanying papers. Not to mention, the statistics faculty were the most prepared for online schooling. They managed to create high-quality learning materials that mimic expensive textbooks in such a small time frame.
